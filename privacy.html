Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Advantage First Financial LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you visit our website www.Adv1st.com (the "Site") or otherwise interact with us online. We are a business-to-business (B2B) marketing agency headquartered in California and provide services nationwide.

1) Information We Collect

A. Information you provide to us: We may collect contact and business information you provide, such as name, business email, business phone number, job title, company name, mailing address, and details submitted through forms, chat, scheduling tools, emails, or phone calls (e.g., marketing needs, campaign goals, budget ranges).

B. Information collected automatically: When you visit the Site, we may automatically collect device and usage data, such as IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system, pages viewed, links clicked, time spent, referring URLs, timestamps, and approximate location derived from IP address.

C. Information from third parties: We may receive information from analytics and advertising partners, scheduling and CRM tools, business/contact enrichment providers (if used), and public sources (e.g., professional networking sites or company websites) when relevant to B2B outreach.

2) How We Use Information

We use information for business purposes such as operating the Site; responding to requests; scheduling calls; providing services; managing client accounts; improving our operations; analytics and performance reporting; marketing our services (B2B); maintaining security; preventing fraud; and complying with legal obligations.

3) How We Disclose Information

We may disclose information to service providers that support our business (e.g., hosting, analytics, CRM, email, scheduling, billing, and security); to professional advisors (legal/accounting); to comply with laws and enforce rights; and in connection with a business transaction (e.g., merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets). We do not disclose personal information to third parties except as described in this Policy.

4) Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., pixels and analytics tags) to improve Site functionality, understand usage, and measure marketing effectiveness. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain features. California notice: If we "sell" or "share" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, we will provide a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" mechanism. 5) SMS/Text Messaging and Opt-In

Opt-in and consent: If you choose to receive SMS/text messages from us (for example, by checking a box on a web form, submitting a request that includes a mobile number with an SMS consent disclosure, or replying with an affirmative keyword), you consent to receive informational and/or marketing text messages at the number you provide. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Message frequency: Message frequency may vary based on your interactions and the services requested.

Opt-out: You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. After you opt out, you may receive a final confirmation message. For help, reply HELP or contact us at info@adv1st.com.

Carrier charges: Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

How we use SMS data: We may use your mobile number and message content to provide requested information, deliver updates, respond to requests, and improve our communications. We may share SMS-related data with service providers who help us send messages (e.g., messaging platforms) and as otherwise described in this Policy.

Mobile information sharing: We do not sell mobile numbers. We do not share mobile numbers with unaffiliated third parties for their own marketing purposes.

6) Data Retention

We retain information as long as reasonably necessary for business purposes such as providing services, maintaining records, complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing agreements. Retention periods vary by data type and legal requirements.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8) Your Privacy Rights (California and Other States)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, deletion of, and correction of certain personal information; to opt out of certain disclosures for advertising; and to not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights. To submit a request, email us at info@adv1st.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We may verify your identity before completing a request. Some privacy laws provide different or limited rights for information collected in a B2B context.

9) Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The Site may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.
